    08

    Cost Management

    Rationalised seats and educated usage

    Seat Rationalisation

    Not every person needs Claude. Gemini Pro is included with Google Workspace for all 223 staff at no additional cost.

    Uncommon needs 80-100 Claude seats, not 223. Gemini Pro covers the rest. Significant cost saving.

    Token Usage: Hidden Cost

    • Opus 4.6 — substantially more tokens than Sonnet
    • Deep Research — large token volumes per session
    • Multiple Opus Deep Research sessions can exhaust monthly allowance in days

    Model Selection Education

    • Sonnet — daily tasks, drafting, brainstorming. Fast and efficient
    • Opus — complex reasoning, nuanced strategy. Use deliberately
    • Deep Research — powerful but expensive. Not a default

    What This Means

    Training must include practical guidance on token-efficient usage. Not just "how to use Claude" but "how to use Claude smartly." This is core to the training programme.
