Cost Management

Rationalised seats and educated usage

Seat Rationalisation

Not every person needs every tool. Uncommon already has Gemini Pro included with Google Workspace, meaning all 223 staff have access to a capable AI assistant at no additional cost.

Claude Enterprise seats should be reserved for power users and departments where advanced capabilities genuinely matter: complex strategy work, custom Skills and Projects, deep research, long-context document analysis, and specialist prompt workflows.

This tiered approach means Uncommon might need 80-100 Claude seats rather than 223, with Gemini Pro covering the rest. That is a significant cost saving.

Token Usage: The Hidden Cost

Claude operates on a fixed monthly token allowance per user. Tokens are consumed with every conversation, and some features burn through them significantly faster than others.

  • Opus 4.6 uses substantially more tokens per interaction than Sonnet
  • Deep Research runs consume large token volumes in a single session
  • A user running multiple Deep Research sessions on Opus could exhaust their monthly allowance in days

The Education Piece

Staff need to understand the difference between models and when to use each one.

  • Sonnet handles the majority of daily tasks quickly and efficiently
  • Opus is for complex reasoning, nuanced strategy work, and tasks requiring the highest capability
  • Deep Research is powerful but expensive and should be used deliberately, not as a default

What This Means for Rollout

Training must include practical guidance on token-efficient usage. Not just "here is how to use Claude" but "here is how to use Claude smartly."

This is a core part of the training programme, not an afterthought.

Gemini Pro // Already Included

Gemini Pro for Light AI Users

Every one of Uncommon's 223 staff already has access to Gemini Pro through Google Workspace at no additional cost. For light AI users, this is more than enough. Here is what Gemini handles well — and where Claude takes over.

Use Gemini For

Everyday AI Tasks

  • Quick drafts and email summaries — fast, integrated with Gmail
  • Meeting prep and notes — pulls from Google Calendar and Docs
  • Basic research and fact-checking — web-grounded answers with sources
  • Document summarisation — massive context window handles 1,000+ page PDFs
  • Brainstorming and ideation — quick lists, alternatives, first-pass thinking
  • Data ingestion at scale — analysing large volumes of PDFs, spreadsheets, or video transcripts
  • Google Workspace integration — native access to Drive, Sheets, Slides, and Docs
Upgrade to Claude For

Advanced Capabilities

  • Deep synthesis and reasoning — connecting dots between complex, contradictory sources
  • Edge case detection — finding subtle logical gaps and lurking variables in data
  • Long-session coherence — context compaction keeps Claude on track across 100+ turn conversations
  • Polished writing and tone — more nuanced, human style for literature reviews and executive summaries
  • Citation reliability — says "I don't know" rather than risking hallucination. Critical for client work
  • Custom Skills and Projects — department-specific AI capabilities loaded on demand
  • Automation pipelines — multi-agent coordination, computer use, and n8n integration

When to Use Each Tool

Task Gemini Pro Claude
Data Ingestion Analysing 1,000+ PDFs or 2-hour videos Deep analysis of 5-10 key papers
Speed / Cost High-volume screening and summaries High-quality drafting and editing
Reasoning Algorithmic and mathematical speed Abstract reasoning and edge-case detection
Workflow Google Workspace / Drive integration Multi-agent coordination and Computer Use
Writing Quick drafts, outlines, first passes Final copy, nuanced tone, client-facing output
Research Broad sweeps, trend scanning, volume Deep synthesis, critique, sourcing integrity

The bottom line: Gemini Pro covers 70-80% of what most staff need on a daily basis, and they already have it. Claude Enterprise seats go to the people who need the remaining 20-30%: deep reasoning, custom Skills, automation, and polished output. This is how you get full agency coverage without 223 Claude licences.
